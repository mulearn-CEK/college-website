About CEK

About College of Engineering Kottarakkara

A premier government-managed technical institution in Kollam, Kerala — committed to excellence in engineering education since 2004.

🏛️ College Campus — Aerial or Front View Add a wide campus photo showing the main building or entrance

The College of Engineering Kottarakkara (CEK) is a distinguished technical institution, established in 2004 under the Institute of Human Resources Development (IHRD). It is approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) and affiliated with APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University (KTU).

Located in Kottarakkara, Kollam, CEK enjoys excellent connectivity in terms of transportation, communication, and accommodation. The campus is part of the National Knowledge Network (NKN) and features a digitally enabled learning environment with smart classrooms and ICT facilities.

Beyond academics, CEK promotes a vibrant culture through various student clubs, technical forums, innovation cells, and professional chapters such as IEEE, ISTE, and CSI. The Innovation and Entrepreneurship Development Cell (IEDC) fosters creativity, leadership, and entrepreneurial skills among students.

The institution has a media centre where video sessions on subjects by experts from premier institutes are screened. Audio-visual aids are thoroughly used in theory and tutorial sessions. Students are encouraged to work on community service projects alongside faculty.
