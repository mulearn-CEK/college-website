About CEK

A Legacy of Engineering Excellence Since 2004

College of Engineering Kottarakkara (CEK) is a distinguished technical institution, established in 2004 under the Institute of Human Resources Development (IHRD). It is approved by AICTE and affiliated with APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University (KTU).

Located in Kottarakkara, Kollam, CEK is well-connected by road, rail, and offers modern infrastructure including smart classrooms, ICT facilities, and National Knowledge Network (NKN) connectivity. The campus fosters a vibrant culture through student clubs, technical forums, and innovation cells.
