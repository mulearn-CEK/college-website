Academics

Programs & Departments

Four undergraduate programs across engineering, computer applications, and management — designed for the careers of tomorrow.

Programs

Degree Programs Offered

All programs are full-time, affiliated to APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University (KTU), and approved by AICTE.

Engineering & Technology

B.Tech Computer Science & Engineering

A comprehensive 4-year program covering algorithms, data structures, software engineering, databases, networking, and modern computing paradigms. Students gain hands-on experience through lab work, projects, and industrial visits.

Intake: 120 Seats Duration: 4 Years Level: UG NRI: Yes
Engineering & Technology

B.Tech CSE (Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning)

A specialised 4-year program focused on AI, deep learning, natural language processing, computer vision, and intelligent systems. Students work with modern ML frameworks and real-world datasets.

Intake: 60 Seats Duration: 4 Years Level: UG NRI: Yes
Computer Application

Bachelor of Computer Application (BCA)

A 4-year program covering programming languages, database management, web technologies, software development, and IT infrastructure. Prepares students for careers in software development and IT services.

Intake: 60 Seats Duration: 4 Years Level: UG University: KTU
Management

Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA)

A 4-year program in business management, entrepreneurship, marketing, finance, and organisational behaviour. Designed to develop future business leaders and entrepreneurs.

Intake: 60 Seats Duration: 4 Years Level: UG University: KTU

Departments

Academic Departments

Our academic community spans engineering, science, and management disciplines.

Computer Science & Engineering

Offers B.Tech CSE and B.Tech CSE (AI & ML) programs. The department has dedicated labs for computing, hardware, and AI/ML research with modern infrastructure.

Electronics & Communication Engineering

Four specialised labs: Communication/Digital Lab, Electronics Circuits Lab, Microcontroller Lab, and DSP Lab with modern test and measurement equipment.

Department of Applied Science

Provides foundational courses in Mathematics, Physics, and English. Faculty include experienced educators with doctoral qualifications and published research.

General Department

Offers courses in Physical Education, Electrical Engineering, and Mechanical Engineering workshops. Supports holistic student development through sports and practical training.

Learning

Academic Framework

A structured semester-based system with continuous evaluation, industry exposure, and research opportunities.

📚 Classrooms & Labs Add photos of smart classrooms, computer labs, or library

Evaluation System

Semester-wise examinations for all semesters. Internal assessment includes two series exams per semester, assignments, and surprise tests. Results declared every six months.

Teaching-Learning

Audio-visual aids, EDUSAT interactive sessions, expert video screenings, industrial visits, community service projects, and psychological counselling for holistic development.

Library & Digital Resources

Central library with 5,990 book volumes, 12 national and 161 international print journals. Digital library with full IEEE & IET journals (IEL Online, 1-user) and NPTEL video courses.

Career Development

Training and Placement Cell (TPC) conducts aptitude tests, group discussions, mock interviews, and career counselling. Strong alumni network supports placements and higher studies.
