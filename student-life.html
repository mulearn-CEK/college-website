Student Life

Life at CEK

Beyond the classroom — a vibrant campus community built on innovation, leadership, and service.

Clubs & Forums

Student Organisations

From technical chapters to service wings, CEK offers diverse platforms for students to explore, create, and lead.

📡 IEEE Event

IEEE Student Branch

The IEEE student branch at CEK conducts technical talks, workshops, exhibitions, and student outreach programmes at schools. Members gain access to global IEEE resources, networking opportunities, and professional development.

💡 IEDC Hackathon

IEDC — Innovation Cell

The Innovation and Entrepreneurship Development Cell (IEDC) fosters creativity, leadership, and entrepreneurial skills. CEK has won prizes at cluster-level hackathons. The cell also received grants from Kerala Startup Mission.

🤖 REACT Event

REACT — Technical Forum

A student-run technical forum focused on robotics, automation, coding challenges, and emerging technologies. REACT organises hackathons, project showcases, and peer-learning sessions throughout the year.

🌿 NSS Camp

NSS — National Service Scheme

The NSS unit at CEK engages students in community service, environmental awareness campaigns, health drives, and social outreach. Students develop leadership skills while making a tangible impact in local communities.

🎨 Arts Fest

Arts & Sports Clubs

The college has active arts and sports clubs organising annual arts festivals, sports meets, inter-collegiate tournaments, essay competitions, debates, and literary events through the extra-mural club.

📰 Magazine

College Magazine & Newsletter

The college publishes a periodic magazine and newsletter showcasing student achievements, research, creative writing, and campus updates. Students contribute as editors, writers, and designers.

Support

Student Services

Comprehensive support services ensuring every student thrives academically, professionally, and personally.

🎯 Training & Placement Cell

The TPC prepares students for competitive examinations and interviews through intensive training encompassing aptitude tests, group discussions, mock interviews, and behavioural psychology sessions. Career counselling is provided periodically.

🧠 Professional Counselling

Regular sessions with a professionally qualified counsellor for stress management, emotional intelligence development, and personal growth. Psychological analysis-based counselling helps students navigate academic and personal challenges.

📚 Digital Library

Full access to IEEE and IET journals through IEL Online digital library. NPTEL video courses, reference texts, and online resources supplement classroom learning. 5,990 book volumes available in the central library.

🏠 Hostel & Amenities

Boys hostel (40 capacity) and girls hostel (42 capacity) with reading rooms, recreation facilities, and medical support. Cafeteria with seating for 50, indoor and outdoor sports facilities, and a gymnasium.
