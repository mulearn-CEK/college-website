How We Prepare

Placement Process

From day one, our holistic training framework ensures every student is equipped for the professional world.

01

Aptitude Training

Quantitative, logical reasoning, and verbal ability sessions integrated into the academic schedule.

02

Technical Workshops

Domain-specific training, coding bootcamps, and hands-on labs aligned with industry requirements.

03

Mock Interviews

One-on-one mock interviews with industry professionals and faculty panelists.

04

Industry Connect

Campus drives, company visits, guest lectures, and networking with alumni recruiters.
