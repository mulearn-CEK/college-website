Process

How to Apply

Admission is through the Centralised Allotment Process (CAP) conducted by the Commissioner for Entrance Examinations (CEE), Kerala.

01

Check Eligibility

Minimum 50% marks in Mathematics and 50% in Physics, Chemistry & Mathematics combined in qualifying examination. Reservations as per Government norms.

02

Apply to CEE Kerala

Register for the centralised admission process through the Commissioner for Entrance Examinations (CEE), Kerala website. Submit required documents.

03

Participate in CAP

Based on merit rank, participate in the Centralised Allotment Process. Select CEK as your preferred institution. Confirm admission by paying the fee.

Fee & Scholarships

Fee Structure & Waivers

Affordable education with government-regulated fee structure and AICTE tuition fee waiver scheme for eligible students.

Fee Structure (Annual)

  • State Merit: ₹35,000 tuition fee
  • Management Quota: ₹65,000 tuition fee
  • NRI Quota: ₹1,00,000 tuition fee
  • Admission Fee: ₹500 (one time)
  • Caution Deposit: ₹5,000 (refundable)
  • University Fees: ₹6,500 (approx.)

AICTE Tuition Fee Waiver

Eligible students can avail the AICTE Tuition Fee Waiver Scheme. The college offers:

  • 10% supernumerary seats under fee waiver
  • Covered under AICTE norms
  • Merit-based selection within each category
  • Previous waivers awarded to eligible candidates

Seat Distribution

Program Intake & Quotas

100% merit quota (State Merit) with reservations as per Government of Kerala norms. All seats filled from the rank list prepared by CEE Kerala.

CSE (120 Seats)

B.Tech Computer Science & Engineering — full-time, 4-year program. NRI quota available.

AICTE EoA 2026–27: Approved intake of 120 seats.

CSE AI & ML (60 Seats)

B.Tech CSE with specialisation in Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning. NRI quota available.

AICTE EoA 2026–27: Approved intake of 60 seats.

BCA (60 Seats)

Bachelor of Computer Application — 4-year program affiliated to KTU. NRI quota not available.

AICTE EoA 2026–27: Approved intake of 60 seats.

BBA (60 Seats)

Bachelor of Business Administration — 4-year program affiliated to KTU. NRI quota not available.

AICTE EoA 2026–27: Approved intake of 60 seats.
